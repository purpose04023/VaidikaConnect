## 1. Description of Service
VaidikaConnect acts as an enabling aggregator platform connecting users (devotees) with verified Hindu Purohits (priests) for the performance of sacred Vedic rituals and ceremonies.

## 2. Pure Agent Clause (GST & Taxation)
VaidikaConnect operates strictly as a **"Pure Agent"** under Indian GST Law:
* **Priest Dakshina**: The transaction value designated as Priest Dakshina is collected by VaidikaConnect on behalf of the customer and passed through in full to the officiating Purohit. This amount is GST-exempt and does not constitute gross platform revenue.
* **Platform Convenience Fee**: VaidikaConnect charges a 5% platform fee for coordinate matching, logistics coordination, and reminder services. This platform fee is subject to 18% GST.
* **TDS Compliance**: Under Section 393(1), a 0.1% TDS is deducted for Purohits whose cumulative platform revenue exceeds ₹5,00,000 in the current financial year.
