# Privacy Policy - VaidikaConnect

**Effective Date: April 1, 2026**

At VaidikaConnect, we value your privacy and are committed to safeguarding your personal data in accordance with applicable laws, including Indian information technology rules.

## 1. Information We Collect
* **Personal Identity Information**: Name, Email Address, Phone Number, WhatsApp Number, Gotra, Nakshatra, and Ancestral details (such as Death Dates for Tithi tracking).
* **Location Data**: Latitude and Longitude coordinates used strictly to match you with nearby regional Purohits.
* **Transaction Details**: Details of bookings, priest Dakshina, platform fees, and payment confirmation status.

## 2. How We Use Your Information
* To match you deterministically with officiating Purohits based on sect, language, and geo-proximity.
* To schedule and dispatch automated ancestral Tithi reminders via WhatsApp.
* To coordinate billing, process taxes, and generate GST-compliant invoices.
* To resolve disputes and coordinate support tickets when escalated.

## 3. Data Retention and Sharing
* We do not sell or rent your data.
* We share necessary identity and contact details with the assigned Purohit for ritual coordination.
* We share transaction details with regulatory authorities to comply with taxation laws (including TDS and GST reporting).

For support or questions, reach out to support@vaidikaconnect.com.
